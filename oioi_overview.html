OIOI Overview

The “National Olympiad in Informatics” competition, or as it is called the “Programming Olympiad” - is the local version in the Sultanate of the “International Olympiad in Informatics” competition. It is a competition that is spread around the world in more than 90 countries that recently participated in the final of the World Olympics, which was held in the Republic of Azerbaijan during the period 7 - 14 August 2022.

The idea of ​​the Olympiad is for general education school students to compete in solving a set of real-life problems that require the design of computer algorithms to be applied as computer programs during the competition sessions using one of the high-end programming languages ​​such as Python, Java, and C++.
