OCPC Overview

The idea of ​​the competition revolves around three-person teams of students from the Sultanate’s colleges and universities competing in solving a set of real-life problems that require the design of computer algorithms to be applied as computer programs during the competition sessions using one of the high-end programming languages ​​such as Python, Java, and C++.

competition Stratgy

The competition (whether national or international) is held in one session that lasts 5 continuous hours, so that the teams are given the same number of questions, varying in their levels, and the teams must solve the largest number of questions within the specified period. At the end of the competition, the results of the participating teams are calculated based on the number of correct solutions, the speed of solution for each problem, and the minimum number of attempts to solve each problem.

OCPC Objectives

  • Discover and develop young talents in programming.
  • Foster logical thinking skills.
  • Enhance problem-solving abilities.
  • Promote algorithm design skills.
  • Enable software development in line with technical progress.
  • Elevate the Sultanate in technology production for smart solutions and applications.
  • Exploit intelligence industry and Fourth Industrial Revolution technologies.
  • Improve production lines at all levels.
