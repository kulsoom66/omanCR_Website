About Us

We are a passionate and dedicated team of tech enthusiasts from Sultant Of Oman. Through hackathons, coding competitions, workshops, and collaborative projects, we aim to provide a platform where individuals from all backgrounds can come together to learn, innovate, and push the boundaries of what's possible in the digital world. Join us on this exciting journey as we explore the limitless possibilities of code and drive positive change in Oman's tech ecosystem. Together, we code for a brighter future!

OCPC Competition

Oman Collegiate Programming Contest (OCPC) is an annual programming competition held in the Sultanate of Oman. It serves as a platform for students from universities and colleges to showcase their programming and problem-solving skills. Read more

BEAVER Competition

Beaver Challenge is a global program that has a collection of short online problems which seeks to foster the understanding of informatics and computational thinking among students of various ages in schools worldwide. Read more

IOI Competition

International Olympiad in Informatics is an annual international programming competition for secondary school students, where participants solve different problems. It's one of the most prestigious competitions in the field of competitive programming. Read more
