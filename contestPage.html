The Omani Olympiad in Informatics competition “Olympiad of Programming” is represented by students of general education schools competing to solve a set of life problems that need to design computerized algorithms, to be applied as computer programs during the competition sessions using a programming language.

What is the Beaver Challenge?

The Beaver Challenge is a global program that seeks to foster the understanding of informatics and computational thinking among students of various ages in schools worldwide. With a presence in more than 50 countries and a participation of 3 million students, this initiative is specifically designed to ignite enthusiasm for the field of computing in every student.

Competition Mechanism

It is a collection of short online problems, called Beaver Challenge. The tasks are fun, engaging and interactive. The tasks can be solved without prior knowledge, but instead require logical thinking.

Beaver's Goals

Enhances the ability to solve problems
Testing student's capacity for problem-solving
Learn indirect ways to use methodical solutions
Resolve as many entries as you can in the time provided.
Practice with Beaver

The Beaver Quiz is made up of a collection of quick online exercises called Beaver Quests. The assignments are enjoyable and interesting and are based on issues that computer scientists frequently encounter and take pleasure in resolving. The tasks can be completed without prior knowledge, although logical reasoning is needed. The competition's goal is to complete as many tasks as you can in the allocated time.
